About me

I’m a security and privacy researcher at Two Six Technologies in Arlington, VA. My research interests include measuring the privacy risks of everyday consumer products and services, user perceptions of security issues, and mobile platform security. In my free time, I enjoy board games, biking, and annoying the neighbors with my ukulele and radio voice.
